# THE-PREVALENCE-AND-FACTORS-ASSOCIATED-WITH-DEPRESSION-AND-ANXIETY-AMONG-ADOLESCENTS-IN-EDO-STATE
This study examines how common depression and anxiety are among adolescents in Edo State and the factors linked to them, such as family problems, school stress, social pressure, and economic hardship. The findings aim to support early help, prevention, and better mental health care for young people.
